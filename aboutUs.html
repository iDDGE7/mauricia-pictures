Our history

In 2008, Raino Mauricia founded Mauricia Pictures by making his hobby his daily work. He started working from home, editing videos and capturing parties, weddings and communions. After a short while, his work was noticed by a larger audience. Since then, Raino has overseen the growth of Mauricia Pictures from a small business to the full-blown video production company it is today. Besides being the Managing Director, he still operates as a camera man, producer and editor.
